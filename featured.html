Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?
First, add your church for free using the "Add Your Church" form. Once your listing is approved, email us at brandonkz@gmail.com to upgrade to Featured or Premium.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! No contracts. Cancel anytime with 7 days notice. Your listing will remain as a free listing after cancellation.
How do I pay?
We accept EFT, SnapScan, or monthly debit order. Contact us at brandonkz@gmail.com to set up payment.
What makes a listing "Featured"?
Featured listings appear at the top of search results with a blue highlighted border and "FEATURED" badge. They get 3-5x more views than regular listings.
Can I update my listing details?
Yes! Email us anytime at brandonkz@gmail.com with updates to your service times, contact details, or other information. Premium listings get a self-service dashboard for instant updates.
Do you offer discounts for multiple locations?
Yes! If your church has multiple campuses, contact us for multi-location pricing. We offer discounts starting at 2+ locations.
